S.D. Overdrive

Management:

Second Dose is managed by S.D. Overdrive, whose approach combines decisive planning, unapologetic confidence, and a firm belief that momentum improves with volume.
Overdrive focuses on direction, pacing, and forward motion, favoring action over hesitation and clarity over caution.

When not attending to his responsibilities as President of FIRA (Federation of International Rock Advancement), he will be clutching the promotional reins of Second Dose, while ensuring the band operates at full throttle. "WFO" as he puts it.

Expectations are simple: move fast, commit fully, and when in doubt... blow it up.

— S.D. Overdrive
