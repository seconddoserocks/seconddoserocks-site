Volume First. Questions Later.

December 16, 2025

It’s official. I’m the manager of Second Dose.

This was not put to a vote. There was no roundtable. No focus group. At some point, momentum required a steering wheel, and I picked it up.

Management, for the record, does not mean spreadsheets and soft landings. It means direction. It means pacing. It means making decisions quickly enough that doubt doesn’t get a seat at the table.

Second Dose doesn’t need permission. It needs movement. Releases. Noise. Pressure. Forward motion.

If you’re wondering whether this will make things more organized — yes, absolutely. If you’re wondering whether it will make things quieter — you’ve misunderstood the assignment.

The plan is simple: move fast, commit fully, and when in doubt, turn it up until the answer becomes obvious.

Welcome to management.

— S.D. Overdrive
